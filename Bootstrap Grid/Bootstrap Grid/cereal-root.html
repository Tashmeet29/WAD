Cereal Root Recipe

This hearty root vegetable dish is perfect for a winter meal.




Ingredients

  • 1 lb cereal root, peeled and diced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups vegetable broth

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Toss the diced cereal root with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Roast in the oven for 25-30 minutes until tender and golden brown.
  4. Remove from oven and place in a pot with vegetable broth.
  5. Cook over medium heat for 10 minutes. Serve warm.
