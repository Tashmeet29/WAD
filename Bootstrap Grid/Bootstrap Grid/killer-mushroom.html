Killer Mushroom Recipe

This hearty root vegetable dish is perfect for a winter meal.




Ingredients

  • 1 lb mushrooms, cleaned and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add mushrooms and cook until golden brown, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Season with salt, pepper, and minced garlic. Cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped parsley.
  5. Serve hot as a side dish or over pasta.
